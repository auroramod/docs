Updating IW7-Mod

IW7-Mod has a auto-updater that runs when the client is open. This applies to dedicated servers too to ensure everyone stays up to date and doesn't fall behind.

Toggling updates offâ€‹

warning

We do not recommend turning updates off for IW7-Mod unless you are on your own build and developing.

To turn the auto updater off, add -noupdate as a command line parameter passed through to the exe via Command Prompt or a .bat file.
